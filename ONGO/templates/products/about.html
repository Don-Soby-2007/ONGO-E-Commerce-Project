What's Inside

Project Features

A full-featured e-commerce experience built with real-world complexity in mind.

Product Search

Filter and search across the full product catalog by name, category, and price.

Secure Authentication

Email + password login, OTP verification, and Google OAuth 2.0 sign-in.

Shopping Cart

Persistent cart with real-time item count, quantity management, and subtotals.

Checkout System

Full checkout flow with address selection, coupon entry, and payment simulation.

Coupon System

Apply discount coupons with percentage or fixed-amount offers at checkout.

Admin Dashboard

Analytics charts, sales reports, order management, and inventory controls.

Offer Management

Product and category-level offers with priority control and time-bound activation.

Wallet System

In-platform wallet with credit/debit transactions and refund processing.
